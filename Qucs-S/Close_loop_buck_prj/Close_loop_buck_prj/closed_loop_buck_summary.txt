- Target Output Voltage (VOUT): 5 V
- Switching Frequency: 100 kHz
- Inductor (L): 100 µH (with 0.01 Ohm ESR added for convergence damping)
- Output Capacitor (COUT): 100 µF (with 0.05 Ohm ESR)
- Initial Load: 10 Ω

2. Controller Method:
A simplified behavioral feedback loop was implemented. VOUT is fed back via the FB node and compared to a 5 V VREF using a VCVS error amplifier (Gain = 20). The amplified error signal is compared against a 100 kHz sawtooth carrier wave using a B-source behavioral comparator. A small RC filter (100 Ohm, 100 pF) smooths the digital PWM edge to prevent infinite dv/dt simulator crashes. The resulting PWM signal drives a floating VCVS gate driver.
